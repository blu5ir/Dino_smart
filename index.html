[ SECTION 05: RESOLUTIONS ]

QUERY LOGS.

[ PAGE 18 ]

Q: Why are there no colors or dark-mode toggles?

A: We designed Dino Smart for absolute visual stability. Color creates unconscious hierarchies and cognitive friction. Pure black and white mimics the print medium, reducing screen glare and focusing attention entirely on readability.

Q: Can I import PDFs and image files?

A: Yes. The backend parses documents and converts scanned handwritten notes via image OCR, formatting them into pure text sheets and structured study guides.

[ PAGE 19 ]

Q: How does the dinosaur mascot react?

A: The dinosaur companion reacts physically as you scroll and interact. It will blink, shift its posture, and display helpful hints in hand-sketched bubbles, acting as a calm, humanized study companion.

Q: Is there an offline/printed version?

A: Yes. Every guide, revision notebook, and sheet generated includes custom-designed print CSS so it exports perfectly to physical A4 paper formats.
